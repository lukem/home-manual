# Maintenance intervals

Various components of the home need regular maintenance.

| **Item**                     | **Interval**                | **Section**                                                                                     |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Air purifier filters         | Device notification         | [Air purifiers](Appliances.md#air-purifiers)                                                  |
| Battery damage inspection    | 3 months                    | [Battery energy storage system](Energy.md#battery-energy-storage-system)                  |
| Battery long term inspection | 2 years                     | [Battery energy storage system](Energy.md#battery-energy-storage-system)                  |
| Battery system test          | 5 years | [Battery energy storage system](Energy.md#battery-energy-storage-system)                  |
| Clocks                       | DST changeover              | [Appliances](Appliances.md), including: Dishwasher, Dryer, Microwave, Oven, Steam Oven, and Washing Machine. |
| Garage roller door           | Device notification         | [Roller door](Garage.md#roller-door)                                                          |
| Garden watering settings     | Monthly                     | [Garden controller](Garden.md#garden-controller)                                              |
| Reverse cycle intake         | DST changeover              | [Reverse cycle air conditioner](Heating-and-cooling.md#reverse-cycle-air-conditioner) |
| Sewer pipes                  | As required, usually annual | [Sewer pipes](Water-and-sewer.md#sewer-pipes)                                             |
| Smoke alarm test             | DST changeover              | [Smoke alarms](Energy.md#smoke-alarms)                                                        |
| Smoke alarm replace          | 10 years | [Smoke alarms](Energy.md#smoke-alarms)                                                        |
| Solar PV system test       | 5 years | [Solar PV](Energy.md#solar-pv)                        |
| Water filters                | Annual                      | [Water filters](Water-and-sewer.md#water-filters)                                         |
